🕹️ Gameplay Mechanics

Your objective is simple: survive infinite waves, destroy enemy ships, defeat powerful bosses, and accumulate a high score.

  • ⌨️
    Desktop Controls Use WASD or Arrow Keys for seamless, precision strafing. Auto-firing is always engaged.
  • 📱
    Mobile Ready Take the fight anywhere. Fully supported touch-and-drag mechanics with an intuitive, dynamic virtual floating joystick!
  • ⚡
    Precision Locked 60 FPS Physics The engine is strictly locked to exactly 60 ticks per second. This guarantees bullet-hell movement remains flawlessly identical whether you play on a 60Hz phone or a 144Hz desktop monitor!

🌍 Online Co-Op Multiplayer

Grab a friend and survive the bullet hell together! The game features a fully custom, peer-to-peer WebRTC networking engine built from scratch for frictionless real-time multiplayer.

  • 🔗
    Zero-Config Matchmaking No servers, no logins. Just click "Online Co-Op", generate an invite link, and send it to your wingman to jump squarely into the action.
  • ⚡
    Host-Authoritative Sync The Host handles all physics and hit-detection locally, streaming precise game state frames to the Client to prevent desynchronization.
  • 🤝
    Shared Loot & Boss Fights Health drops, weapon buffs, and intense boss fights are fully synchronized, turning single-player bullet hells into strategic coordinated assaults.

❤️ The Player's Defenses

Surviving the bullet hell requires mastering your defenses and managing your health pool.

  • ♥
    Health Points The player starts with a base health of 6 hearts. Taking damage depletes them.
  • 🛡
    Shields Acts as over-armor. When collected, it visually surrounds the ship and takes damage before hearts do.
  • ✨
    Invincibility Frames After taking damage, the ship temporarily flickers, granting brief invincibility.

🚀 Weapon Systems & Upgrades

The game features a dynamic auto-firing weapon system with 7 distinct gun levels. Guns picked up during waves last for 15 seconds.

  • ▲
    1. Single Shot The standard base setup.
  • ▲▲
    2. Twin Shot Shoots two parallel streams.
  • ❋
    3. Spread Shot 3-way spread for side-coverage.
  • ⚡
    4. Laser A continuous, high-damage golden beam.
  • ★
    5. Storm Shot A 5-way wide fan spread that decimates groups.
  • 🚀
    6. Rocket Salvo High-explosive rockets with area-of-effect (AoE) damage.
  • 🎯
    7. Homing Missiles Advanced AI-guided rockets that seek and destroy the nearest targets.

Defeating Boss 1 permanently upgrades your base gun to Twin Shot. Defeating Boss 2 permanently upgrades it to Spread Shot!

💎 Drops & Pick-ups

Players are rewarded with various dynamic drops from defeating enemies or damaging bosses.

  • ▲
    Gun Tokens Upgrades the player's firing rate and pattern to a specific gun level.
  • ♥
    Heart Packs Restores 1 lost health point.
  • 🛡
    Shield Orbs Adds an extra layer of shield armor.
  • ⚡
    Invincible Packs Grants a golden glow and 3 seconds of total invulnerability to shred enemies.

🛸 Enemy Variants & Hazards

The game features highly dynamic spawn waves with distinct enemy types that require different strategies to counter.

  • Small/Medium Ships Standard grunts. Low health but appear in vast numbers.
  • Speeders Fast-moving interceptors that flank quickly from the edges.
  • Tanks Heavily armored bruisers shooting wide spreads of intimidating projectiles.
  • Snipers Elusive hovering ships that fire ultra-fast precision projectiles.

Projectile Damage Profiles

  • Standard Orange Plasma: Deals exactly 1 Heart (or Shield) of damage.
  • Heavy Red Orb: Fired by Tanks and Bosses. Deals 2 Hearts (or Shields) of damage.
  • Boss Laser: Massive targeted beam. Shaves off 3 entire Hearts immediately!
  • Random Environmental Laser: Instant One-Shot Kill! DO NOT TOUCH.

☠️ Epic Boss Fights

Every 5th wave is a Boss Wave, heavily ramping up the difficulty.

  • Alpha Guardian A massive, heavily armored dreadnought that fires dense, alternating multi-directional bursts. Requires careful weaving.
  • Crimson Reaper An aggressive figure-8 moving interceptor. Possesses menacing double glowing eyes and fires rapid, tight bullet-hell spirals.
  • Void Sovereign A colossal purple dreadnought with a Demonic V-eye slit and Hellfire core. Sweeping charge attacks and devastating spread fire.
  • Iron Colossus A colossal carrier fortress bristling with 2 rotating turrets and 2 rear cannons — all independently destructible. Destroy the turrets to silence their aimed fire, take out the cannons to stop the downward spread, then finish the hull. Spawns at Wave 20.
